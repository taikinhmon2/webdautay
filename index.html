Liliana's biography

She had traveled the world and seen much, from the earliest chaos to the appearance of more and more fantastic and wonderful creatures that came in a variety of forms, creatures that were truly of this world, unlike proto-creatures like Liliana.The sleeping creature before her, however, was more remarkable than any that she had seen.It had a wonderful, luscious white fur, with nine tails swaying gently behind it like leaves in the wind.Liliana was fascinated, and she began to travel with the Nine-Tails that dwelled in the ancient kingdom of dragons.
